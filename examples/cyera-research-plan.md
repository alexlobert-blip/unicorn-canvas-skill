## 3. Question packs by research type

### Customer call — CISO at Fortune-2000

1. **In your own words, what does Cyera do for you?** — *why-asked: validates brand line + surfaces customer paraphrases.*
2. **What would your CIO say Cyera does, if asked at a board meeting?** — *why-asked: tests the brand line's surface-durability across upward-facing surfaces.*
3. **Why did you pick Cyera over [Wiz / Varonis / status quo]?** — *why-asked: Pain 2 differentiation; surfaces the specific comparison language.*
4. **Tell me about the moment Cyera saved you time or pain — what was the before/after?** — *why-asked: Outcomes layer; pulls numeric stories with named context.*
5. **Would you be willing to be cited in a case study with the [specific number]?** — *why-asked: gates Outcomes layer for sales-ready usage.*
6. **Where does Cyera still feel like work for you?** — *why-asked: surfaces module gaps and emerging pains for Pain 3.*
7. **If you'd never heard of Cyera, what would you Google to find them?** — *why-asked: tests brand line + pain language match.*

### Analyst interview — Gartner / Forrester / IDC

1. **Where does Cyera sit in the DSPM landscape vs. Wiz, Varonis, Microsoft Purview?** — *why-asked: validates competitive position for Differentiation Pain 2.*
2. **Which buyer pain is most urgent right now for the Fortune-2000 CISO?** — *why-asked: confirms dominant pain ranking.*
3. **What category language is winning in CISO budget conversations this quarter?** — *why-asked: sharpens brand line + pain language.*
4. **What's the biggest contrarian move you've seen in this space in the last 12 months?** — *why-asked: tests whether Cyera's positioning is brave enough.*
5. **Where is the AI-data-exposure pain heading in 12 months?** — *why-asked: future-proofs Pain 1 and the emerging pain selection.*

### Field expert validation — Practicing CISO / CDO

1. **In your role, would you experience Pain 1 (AI data exposure) without experiencing Pain 2 (SaaS access governance)?** — *why-asked: MECE test on the three pains.*
2. **If someone gave you Cyera's brand line as a hero, would you click through?** — *why-asked: brand line surface-durability test.*
3. **For the Pain 3 audit-prep motion, what trigger would tell you a customer is ready to talk?** — *why-asked: validates power-play trigger detectability.*
4. **What would make this canvas's modules look fake to a buyer?** — *why-asked: stress-tests the SKU-shaped Modules constraint.*

### Sales / GTM alignment

1. **Walk me through your last 3 closed-won deals — which of the three pains drove each?** — *why-asked: validates the dominant pain selection against revenue reality.*
2. **For Pain 2 SaaS access governance — do you have the muscle to run a `[NEW MOTION]` play next quarter?** — *why-asked: validates Power Play readiness.*
3. **Which module do you actually lead demos with for each pain?** — *why-asked: surfaces wedge module per column.*
4. **When buyers compare us to Wiz / Varonis on objection calls, what differentiation language wins?** — *why-asked: validates Differentiation Pain 2 language.*

### CS validation

1. **Which 3 customers per pain are willing to be cited with [specific number]?** — *why-asked: gates Outcomes layer for sales-ready usage.*
2. **For the 14d → 90min, 41%, and 6wk → 4d numbers — are they still current?** — *why-asked: validates outcome freshness.*
3. **Which customers are quietly NOT happy with the modules we list under Pain 1?** — *why-asked: stress-tests Modules column.*
